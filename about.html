About me

Hello, I'm Farzaneh, a passionate UX designer with a background in architecture and urban planning, bringing a unique perspective to creating seamless and enjoyable user experiences. I enjoy simplifying people's lives through intuitive design solutions, making their day-to-day interactions more pleasant.

My journey into UX design was inspired by my natural curiosity about how users interact with their surroundings, whether in physical spaces or digital interfaces. With several years of experience, I have honed my skills in developing user-centered designs that not only captivate visually but also excel in functionality. My background has instilled in me a keen eye for understanding the complexities of user interactions within diverse environments.

My favourite way to spend my free time is exploring new cultures and new cuisines. Living in three different countries—Iran , Italy, and Germany— I have learned how food shapes and reflects a culture’s identity. Nowadays, I like to learn about traditional foods from all over the world and try to recreate them on my own. Apart from my food adventures, I also love traveling and going swimming.

About me

Hi, I'm Farzaneh, a UX/UI designer blending insights from architecture and urban planning. My focus? Crafting user-centric designs rooted in understanding human behavior. I'm all about keeping things simple and enjoyable. My goal is to craft designs that effortlessly enhance your day-to-day interactions.

My interest in UX design sparked while taking a Service Design course at the Polytechnic University of Milan. The focus was on urban intelligence and how to redesign existing services to align with the emerging perspective of urban smartness. The course provides insights into the intersection of urban design, technology, and citizen engagement that significantly influenced my interest in UX design.

Building upon this interest, I enrolled in a UX Design and UI Specialization course at Careerfoundry, spanning from December 2022 to November 2023. Throughout these intensive training programs, I delved into a realm of research, sketching, information architecture, wireframing, and prototyping for both web and mobile products. These aspects have become fundamental components of my design process, empowering me to develop user-centered designs that not only captivate visually but also excel in functionality.

Beyond UX design, I'm an art enthusiast who loves exploring galleries and museums. The world of art not only captivates me but also plays a significant role in shaping my creative perspective. When not immersed in design, you might catch me nurturing a small jungle of plants, cultivating a peaceful and artful haven within my space.
